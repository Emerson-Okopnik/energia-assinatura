# Marketing UI Kit — consorcioliderenergy.com

Recreation of the Líder Energy public marketing site: single-page lead-gen layout with hero, two audience panes, "Como funciona" steps, FAQ, contact form, and footer.

## Components

- `Header.jsx` — sticky top nav with logo + 4 links + WhatsApp CTA
- `Hero.jsx` — split hero: headline + CTA on the left, lead form on the right
- `AudiencePane.jsx` — the two tall cards for Consumidor / Usineiro
- `HowItWorks.jsx` — 4 numbered illustrated steps
- `FAQ.jsx` — accordion, orange-plus open state
- `ContactStrip.jsx` — pre-footer WhatsApp strip
- `Footer.jsx` — logo, contact info, socials, legal line

## Notes
- The site is Brazilian-Portuguese only.
- The lead form on the public site uses radio-style audience picker — we mirror that.
- The "Como funciona" illustrations on the live site are custom PNGs not provided to us — we've used tasteful Lucide substitutes and flagged this in the main README.
